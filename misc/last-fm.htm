My Last.fm Profile (2012-2015)

Back in the days when Last.fm allowed profile customization and befriending others, I listened to far more music than I do now. I didn't know HTML but I would always customize up my Last.fm sidebar in BBcode.

This was the closest thing I had to a website at the time, so I made this mural/collage with period-correct bits and pieces that I could still salvage from the Internet Archive at different points in time, I hope you'll indulge me while I try to rekindle faded memories for a bit.
